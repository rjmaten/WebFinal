About

What is FlrGrl?
FlrGrl is a style blog dedicated to empowering women around the world by inspiring confidence and creativity through art and fashion. Started in 2018 by siblings Ryan and Lauren Maten, the two bored students decided to take their passion for fashion and turn that into a real fake fashion blog.

"Yeah, sure. That'd be cool."
-Lauren after being asked if she wanted to start a blog.
